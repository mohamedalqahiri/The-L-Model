Upon the activation of the ecosystem and the securing of institutional/sovereign backing, these recorded credits will retroactively convert into official value-units (**Q-Bits**) within the global governance architecture.

### 3. Academic & White Paper Credit
Top-tier contributors who solve critical computational or mathematical synchronization bottlenecks will be formally credited as "Technical Contributors" in the official, globally distributed White Paper of the model.
